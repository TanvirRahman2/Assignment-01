Samsung Galaxy Book Core i5 11th Gen

The Samsung Galaxy Book 15" Laptop comes with an Intel Core i5-1135G7 Processor (8M Cache, 2.40 GHz up to 4.20 GHz). It comes with 8GB of LPDDR4x RAM and a 256GB SSD for fast and efficient storage. The laptop has a 15.6" IPS display with FHD resolution (1920 x 1080) and anti-glare technology. The graphics are handled by Intel Iris Xe Graphics, and the laptop features a Pro Keyboard with a Numeric key Clickpad. It has a 720p HD camera, stereo speakers, and dual-array digital microphones with Dolby Atmos audio enhancement. It also offers security features such as a fingerprint sensor, TPM chip, and a security locker slot. The laptop runs on Windows 10 Pro and is powered by a 54 Wh battery with a 65 W USB Type-C adapter.
